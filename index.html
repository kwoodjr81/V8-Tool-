        How the score works

        The calculator score is a transparent 0–100 rating from three weighted factors:

        • Net margin (50 pts) — what's left after every cost, as a % of price.
        • Profit per sale (30 pts) — dollars left to absorb refunds and bad ad days.
        • Ad efficiency (20 pts) — how much of gross profit ads consume.
        The calculator scores math only. The AI analysis is a qualitative judgment about the product and market — useful for spotting saturation or weak value props, but not a guarantee. Always validate with a real ad test.
